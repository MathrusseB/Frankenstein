The Frank & Stein Story

Two Builders. One Revolution.

In 2005, Frank Pedersen and his longtime friend Steinar Bergby decided the trike industry needed a next generation of rear ends. They adapted decades of design, style, and fabrication experience to create the revolutionary Frankenstein Hot Rod Trike Rear End.

What started as a 30” custom rear end for bike builders grew, by popular demand, into complete bolt-on trike kits for nearly every Harley-Davidson model. Today we build kits that install in a single day — using Dana differentials, 9” Ford street/strip axles, billet 4-piston calipers, stainless steel rotors, and grade 8 hardware. Every Hot Rod Billet and Light Sport kit is still manufactured right here in Pleasanton, Kansas.
