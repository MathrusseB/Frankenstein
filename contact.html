How to Order

Simple Ordering Process.

You can purchase a complete Trike kit designed specifically for your motorcycle, or order individual components to fabricate your own custom trike. Orders can be placed directly with us or through one of our authorized dealers.

To quote your kit, we need the year, make, and model of your motorcycle. Payment in full is required at the time of order.

Shipping & Handling

5 Days to Your Door.

Orders are usually shipped within 5 days. We ship via insured domestic ground delivery to your business or home.

For easy handling, most Trike kits are packaged in 5 separate boxes, protected with expandable foam to avoid shipping damage.

Tracking numbers are provided by phone or email as soon as your kit leaves the shop.
